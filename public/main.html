League of Legends Champions

Head on over to the tracker page to get started!


League of Legends or "LoL" for short is one of the worlds most successful and popular games. As a pc gamer myself it can become very addicting. There is a lot of strategy involved and a champion for everyone. What inspired me for this app was being able to strategize and compile data for each champion. Most importantly the selection phase can determine whether a game will go smoothly or not.


Everyone can contribute to this app by adding a champion to the tracker tab and adding/deleting notes of strengths, weaknesses, item builds, stats, comments, and playstyles in order to gain better knowledge of a character (especially since there are so many to choose from!).


For more information, visit my League of Legends main site.
